What is this about?

This website is one of the assignments called respomnsive portfolio. Which means it's a web development approach that creates dynamic changes to the appearance of a website, depending on the screen size and orientation of the device being used to view it.- google. Basically indicating that when you shrink the website or make any size adjusment to it it will flow with the Y and X of the page. Essentially making the content of the page fit in the desired size you want to have it in. In my website you have a (navbar) on the top right hand side of the page. This element will help you navigate through my portfolio leading you to the main page, About, and the contact section. Also it has a container side where i stored the logo on the top left side. Which I made with the intention that when you click on it it will lead you straight to the main page. Below that I have some main frames with pictures just to demonstrate the responsive side of the page when you shrink it. The pictures are just on the page they wont lead you anywhere, other than that I have a footer on the bottom of the page that covers from left to right, with a instagram logo and a copyright text. This is indeed my first attempt on this and i am quite happy with it, thank you for reading. To see the navbar at more use shrink the page and it will create a drop down navbar.
